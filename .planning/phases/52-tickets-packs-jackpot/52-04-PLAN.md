    6. Click a third pack. EXPECTED: silent.
    7. Reload /play/. Click a pack. EXPECTED: still silent (localStorage persisted the mute).
    8. Toggle the speaker icon back to unmuted. Reload. Click a pack. EXPECTED: sound returns.

       - Crypto quadrant: img src matches `/badges-circular/crypto_{fileIdx}_{name}_{color}.svg` where fileIdx == symbolIdx (1:1).
