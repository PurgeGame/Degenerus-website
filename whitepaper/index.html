Degenerus Protocol

A lottery game where the house takes nothing. The more you play, the better your odds. Earn from every player you bring in, forever. Invest in the game's future and profit as it grows.

What Is Degenerus?

A Lottery

A verifiably fair lottery that returns more than 100% of buyins to participants. Buy tickets to increase your odds of winning the nightly drawings. There's no house edge and the jackpots continuously increase.

An Affiliate Marketing System

Affiliates earn commissions proportional to the ETH their referrals deposit, in perpetuity, three tiers deep. As long as your referrals keep playing, you keep earning.

An Investment for Degenerates

Collectively play the role of the "house", while still individually experiencing the thrill and risks of gambling. Returns are proportional to the speed of the game and length of investment (but primarily determined by LUCK). The variance is intentional; it's what keeps yield farming nits out and reserves the profits for investors who enjoy gambling.

Degenerus Protocol creates value by bringing two types of gamblers together. Lottery players want a shot at tomorrow's big jackpot and accept the vig. Degen investors want to gamble with a positive expectation over a longer time period and are willing to provide capital. Affiliates drive deposits from both sides and earn a share. The largest edges go to players who work all three angles.

The game advances cyclically through levels, with drawings that pay out ticket holders every night. Once the prize pool surpasses the previous level, the Luckbox Jackpots are unlocked, unleashing five days of massive prizes, and restarting the cycle with a higher target.

Profit Is Gated by Contribution

You cannot reliably extract value from Degenerus without first strengthening it. The only paths to profit are bringing in new players, supplying the game with liquidity, or getting lucky. Because this is a solo project with no insiders, everyone is subject to this constraint, equally.

Affiliates only earn when they bring in players who spend. Liquidity providers only profit if the game keeps advancing. Gamblers only reach the next jackpot when the current level fills. Returns are proportional to the speed of the game and level of commitment.

There is no move a player can make that drains the system. Anyone who leaves a winner already earned their returns. The future value they forfeit by quitting rather than reinvesting accrues to the players who stay. Every incentive points in the same, productive direction.

Zero Rake. Negative Rake.

Degenerus Protocol has no "house edge" because the role traditionally played by the house is available to anyone willing to invest or advertise.

But it goes further. All pooled ETH is staked into Lido stETH, earning ~2.5% APR. The staking yield doesn't just go to the creator's vault. Half of it flows back into the prize pools. Half the rest is paid to the $DGNRS community wallet. Free external yield flows into the pools that pay players. Total payouts will eventually exceed total deposits. That's negative rake.

Trustless by Design

The code is law. All contract logic is open-source and immutably deployed on the Ethereum blockchain. Every drawing is determined by a cryptographically secure random number delivered by Chainlink VRF. Altering the rules of the game is absolutely impossible and the admin has no meaningful powers.

Ensuring that every aspect of Degenerus Protocol is 100% fair, transparent and trustless is my top priority.
